About the Inefficiency Index

The inverse inefficiency index is calculated as: 1 / (sum(|log returns|) / |sum(log returns)|)

This {{ window }}-day rolling measure shows the inverse of market efficiency over time, plotted on a secondary y-axis alongside the price. A 14-day Simple Moving Average (SMA) is applied to smooth the index.
